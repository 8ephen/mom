Hormone Replacement Therapy

Provided by Tamara Yoshida, N.P.

At the Office of Richard Malotky, M.D.

Feel Like You Again.

Relief from hormone imbalance is possible — and personal.

Hot flashes. Mood swings. Fatigue. Brain fog. Hormonal changes can affect your body, your emotions, and your daily life — but you do not have to just "deal with it."

Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) is a safe, personalized way to restore hormonal balance and help you feel like you again.

Today's HRT is flexible and designed to fit your lifestyle, with options like:

Creams & Gels

Easy, non-invasive daily application

Pellets

Long-lasting relief with slow-release implants

Injections

Fast-acting and customizable dosing

Patches & Pills

Convenient and familiar formats

With HRT, you may experience:

Fewer hot flashes and night sweats
Improved mood and mental clarity
Better sleep and energy
Healthier skin and libido
Protection for your bones and heart

You have options. You have support.

Contact our office at (530) 244-7707 and schedule an appointment with Tamara Yoshida, N.P. to explore which HRT method is right for your body and your goals.
